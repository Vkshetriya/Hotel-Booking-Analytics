🏨 Hotel Booking Data Analysis & Interactive Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing hotel booking data to uncover key business insights related to customer behavior, revenue generation, and cancellation patterns.

The goal was to transform raw data into actionable insights and present them through an interactive and visually appealing dashboard.

🚀 Key Objectives
Understand booking trends and seasonality
Analyze channel performance (Web, Mobile App, Travel Agent)
Identify patterns in cancellations
Explore customer preferences (room type, stay type, star rating)
Provide business recommendations to improve profitability

🛠️ Tech Stack
Python → Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
Power BI → Data Visualization & Dashboarding
Pandas, NumPy → Data Manipulation
📊 Dashboard Features

✔ KPI Overview (Bookings, Revenue, Profit, Cancellation Rate)
✔ Booking Trends (Seasonality Analysis)
✔ Channel-wise Performance (Revenue & Profit Contribution)
✔ Cancellation Analysis (Time & Channel-based)
✔ Customer Insights (Room Type, Stay Type, Payment Method)
✔ Interactive Filters for dynamic exploration

🔍 Key Insights
📈 Booking demand is highly seasonal with peaks during holidays and year-end
🌐 Direct channels generate the majority of revenue and bookings
❌ Travel agents show the highest cancellation rates
🏨 Standard rooms and 4-star hotels are most preferred
💰 Revenue is concentrated in mid-range offerings (value-for-money segment)
🔁 Higher demand periods are associated with increased cancellations

💡 Business Recommendations
Implement stricter cancellation policies during peak seasons
Promote direct booking channels to maximize profitability
Introduce non-refundable or discounted pricing strategies
Apply dynamic pricing based on demand fluctuations
Reduce dependency on high-cancellation channels
